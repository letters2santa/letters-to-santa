The Santa Claus Letter Corpus project

This project which aims at building the Santa Claus Letter Corpus out of all the letters which were sent to Finland from around the world. These letters feature text and art, mostly handwriting enriched with drawings and were primarily sent by children. The collection, stored at the National Archives of Finland, amounts to 25 shelf meters of letters. The scope of the project is to catalogue and digitize these letters, and from the digitized text build a linguistic corpus.
